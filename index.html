Good To Know

Questions, Answered

How do I book Fantasy Studio for my wedding?

Build your package right here on the site, send it to us on WhatsApp, and your dates are reserved once the 50% advance is paid. The remaining 40% is due on the event day and the final 10% at delivery.

What are the payment terms?

50% advance to confirm your booking, 40% on the event day, and 10% at the time of delivery. These terms are printed on every quote and PDF.

Do you have female photographers for the bridal side?

Yes. We provide dedicated lady shooters for pardah-observing families — a female photographer who covers the ladies’ section exclusively. Lady shooters are ₹8,000 per head per event (₹6,000 photography charge + ₹2,000 female shooter pardah coverage premium). You can add them to any event directly in the Package Builder.

Will we get the raw photos too?

Yes. Raw photos along with the fully edited video are delivered on a pendrive with every package.

Can I customise a ready-made package?

Absolutely. Pick any ready-made package as a starting point, then add or remove services per event — the price updates live as you change things.

How does the premium album work?

Albums are priced at ₹400 per sheet with a minimum of 15 sheets. Choose your sheet count in the builder and the album total is added to your quote instantly.

Which events do you cover?

Manje, Sanchak, Mehndi, Nikah, Ruksati and Valima / Reception — plus engagements and birthdays for single-day coverage.
